Being Fit

Keeping your body fit is most necessary thing to do in life. Even when Astronauts go to space they workout 2.5hrs everyday. As for now keeping body healthy should be a primary concern as if you dont workout or sweat your bone density will decrease and also you will start experiencing weakness at certain parts of your body. In simple words you will have a body of 60+ old person at worst condition. So keeping body healthy is the primary concern. Exercises you can follow up at home to workout at home without any weights or equipments:

  1. One Spot Running
    10 mins
  2. Jumping
    50-100 Reps
  3. Push-UPs
    20-30 Reps
  4. Squats
    15 Reps
  5. Cycling
    5 mins
  6. Leg raise
    20 Reps
  7. Jumping Jacks
    20 Reps
  8. Crunches
    15 Reps
Do these all exercises daily in set of 2 to keep yourself fit.
